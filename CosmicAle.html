Cosmic Ale Branding Concept

The challenge here was to find a startup on KickStarter.com, re-imagine the logo, create a brand manual describing the aspects of the company's visual identity, create graphics for the initial internal stationary (memo & business card samples), and finally wrap it all up in an exclusive package. I chose Cosmic Ale for the opportunity of combining two things I enjoy studying, space and beer. The entire package intends to speak the visual language of a mission brief.
