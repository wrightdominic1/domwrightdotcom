09

THUGGIN'

Freddie Gibbs x Mad Lib

Where "Good Ass Intro" impacted me to be positive, "Thuggin'" pushed me to be a gangsta. When I'd drive myself to jr. college, this would be the song I'd blast, driving through downtown with the windows down. It's a definite confidence booster.

It's also the first rap album on vinyl that I bought with my own money. The packaging is all gold with an embossment of the MC and DJ's names on the front and back. The version I have has Freddie Gibbs' name misspelled as "Freddy."

PRODUCER RELEASE DATE
Otis Jackson Jr. November 21, 2011
