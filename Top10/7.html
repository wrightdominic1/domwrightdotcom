07

C.R.E.A.M.

The Wu Tang Clan

One day snowboarding in the 8th grade, I found an iPod in the snow. The Wu Tang Clan was some of the only rap music on it. My friends had heard of them but it was brand new exposure for me. C.R.E.A.M. was the first song I listened to and after that I declared our friends to be the Wu-Tang Clan. It impacted me because I was able to make friends at a new school with a piece of music. Friends that I still talk to today.

The album I own is a die-cut to be in the shape of the rap group's logo. One of my high school friends picked it up for me on Black Friday, it's the record store day exclusive release.
