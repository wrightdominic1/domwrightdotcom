05

AQUEMINI

OutKast

The only time I cried at a concert was at OutKast. It doesn't make sense. I was just so happy. Going to their concert was like going to church, I felt my sins being cleansed. I knew every word to the songs they were playing and lost it when they played Aquemini. It taught me to take the good with the bad. It also showed me that rap music can be about glamorous vanity like driving Cadillacs, while giving real-life practical advice.

The album I own is the only 3 disc LP in my collection. I questioned why there were only 2-4 songs on each side and I learned that songs with heavier bass have wider grooves when being scratched into a record. Loud, bass heavy, hip hop takes up more space.
