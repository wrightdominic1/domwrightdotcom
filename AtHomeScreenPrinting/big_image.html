What You'll Need


A screen frame
You can make one out of scrap wood or buy a wooden framed picture from goodwill and kick out the picture. Use wood, not metal.

Mesh
You can pick it up from your local art suply store by the foot. Ask for silk screen mesh. They don't carry it at big name chain places like JoAnn's or Michael's so you may need to order it, it's still cheaper than a $60 screen.

And a staple gun, ripe with stapes.
