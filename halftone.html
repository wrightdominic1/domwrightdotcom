Halftone Index

Tasked with creating a catalog about anything except for sale products, I chose to catalog halftone dot patterns. Studying halftones is useful for other areas of my work, it helps with visualizing how on screen graphics look in print. Plus the large scale patterns look really cool. Magazine is 8.5x11 inches, offset printed with a hand screen printed cover. Vol. 2 & vol. 3 coming soon.
