job experience

Okanjo.com

My first time working in an office. Okanjo.com was a startup ecommerce marketplace.

During my time there the first thing I'd do is create a front page banner for the next day, come up with a headline, and search for products to feature in the collection related to the banner I've created. I'd try to get all that done in the first hour of work. see banners

Long term projects included creating email templates, aiding the lead designer in writing the brand manual and defining the visual style of the website. I also participated in whatever daily operations and objectives I could participate in so I could learn as much as I could about working in an office environment.
 

DriveByPress

For the past couple summers and everywhere in-between I've been working for the Drive By Press. They're master printmakers with a shop in Brooklyn, New York. They've devised a way to mobilize woodblock printmaking to make it accessible to print at academic locations all across America.

My role with the Drive By Press was not only to live print shirts for customers but to also take care of the transactions and keep track of inventory. As an event marketing representative I've live printed from places around campus to Lollapalooza in Chicago. Beyond live printing I was also a print assistant during workshops and I designed and printed shirts for local businesses.

Logo Application

Motion Graphics
